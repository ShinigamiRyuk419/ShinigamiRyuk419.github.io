RECYBAGE PROJECT

       This program “ReCybage” is a project that focuses on recycling different kinds of materials that were thrown away, that could be useful in the community. Let’s take for example plastics, cardboards, papers, magazines, and the likes These materials could be used as an alternative for flower pots, decorations in the house, or for any alternative purposes.
       Moreover,this projects would be a great opportunity to raise awareness of the existence of the issues that our environment faces today that really brought into environmental crisis such as the pollution, global warming,overpopulation,waste disposal,ocean acidification,loss of bioderversity,deforestation,ozone aleyer depletion,acid rain,public health issues and so on.

MISSION

       The mission of this program is to ensure the neatness of the environment as well as to develop the creativity and innovativeness of the individual out of the garbages . This program was built for the betterment of our nature in order also to let our future generation can still experience the thing that we experience what we have right now. We create a work environment that values diversity, effective communication, and idea-sharing.We continually evaluate the quality of our programs and services to ensure their efficiency and effectiveness.

VISION

       The Recybage Program envision is to inspire and challenge citizens to achieve the highest waste reduction, recycling, and reuse goals in the nation through innovation and creativity. This program manages waste, recycles resources and protects the environment for any calamities occurs. A programs that improve economic vitality and environmental sustainability. To develop a global brand that will partner with our customers and communities to manage & reduce waste from collection to disposal while recovering valuable resources and creating clean, renewable energy.
OUR STORY

       In addition, this project could lessen the usage of materials that affect our environment in a way that, these materials could cause different types of pollution such as water pollution, air pollution, and also clogging of drainages especially during rainy season and it could also cause the death of animals due to the intake of these materials. This program as well, encourages different social classes to take part of it.
       Furthermore, it would be a significant contribution to make our environment a better place to live in, as future generations would see and feel the same things that we do today. It would be better if we follow the 3R's such reduce,reuse, and recycle.

       The United Nations Foundation is a charitable organization headquartered in Washington, DC, that supports the United Nations and its activities. It was established in 1998 with a $1 billion gift to the United Nations by philanthropist Ted Turner,who believed the UN was crucial for addressing the world's problems.The UN Foundation's main work occurs through building public-private partnerships, communities, initiatives, campaigns, and alliances to broaden support for the UN and solve global problems

       The Ford Foundation is an American private foundation with the stated goal of advancing human welfare. Created in 1936 by Edsel Ford and his father Henry Ford, it was originally funded by a US$25,000 gift from Edsel Ford. By 1947, after the death of the two founders, the foundation owned 90% of the non-voting shares of the Ford Motor Company.Between 1955 and 1974, the foundation sold its Ford Motor Company holdings and now plays no role in the automobile company.

       The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation (BMGF), a merging of the William H.
