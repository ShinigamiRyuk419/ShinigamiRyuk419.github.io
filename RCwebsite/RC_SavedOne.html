SAVED FILES

What we can create from what we use to ignore?

RUBBER PLANT CONTAINER

       From a mere tyre it was transformed into something really useful at the same time it is use as a decoration. Instead of just throwing away those tyres at home or ignoring those on the street why not make it into something new.

CAN PLANT POTS

       Cans these days can be seen everywhere. Instead of throwing it away after consuming its content why not transform it to a simple pot for your plants. Can plant pots is very much useful to organize your indoor plants. You can aslo make it as your pen organizers.

BOTTLE CONTAINER

       Instead of just leaving those plastic bottles floating at the sea or even in everywhere, why not make use of it again. By transforming it into an organizer for your pens and pencils. You can also creatively create a gift container so as not to waste it.

PLASTIC FLOWERS

       After buying lots of stuff at the supermarket we surely have lots of plastic bags. We don't need to throw those, all we have to do is create a simple plastic flowers used as a decoration at your home. There are still lots of ways to creatively recycle.

TIRE CHAIRS

       From a mere tyre it was transformed into a chair that is really very creative and useful. Instead of just throwing away those tyres at home or ignoring those on the street why not make it into something new. Let your imagination work with those garbage.

SPOON VASE

       This work is perfect if you use lots of disposable plastic spoons. Spoon vase are merely made out of those thrown plastic spoon that we usually found just around us. This maybe just a simple act but if you start it now you help save out mother earth.
